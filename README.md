## About

Finance Tracker helps you manage your personal finances with ease — track income and expenses, monitor your wallet balance, and gain insights into your spending habits, all in one clean mobile app.

## Features

- 🔐 **Authentication** — Secure login and account creation
- 📊 **Dashboard** — Overview of your income and expenses at a glance
- 👛 **Wallet** — Track balances across multiple accounts
- ➕ **Transactions** — Add and categorize income or expense entries
- 👤 **Profile** — Manage your account and app settings
